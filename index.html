Bridge End Inn

Ruabon

Nestled in the heart of Ruabon, North Wales, just a stone's throw from the Ruabon train station, The Bridge End Inn is a cosy, rustic, and traditional real ale pub that serves as the beating heart of our local community. Since 2008, the McGivern family—Gill, Peter, and their son Matthew—have been proudly running this beloved establishment, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all who step through our doors.

At The Bridge End Inn, we are passionate about real ale. With our very own micro-brewery on-site, Matthew occasionally brews unique, small-batch beers that you can only find at our pub. We’re proud to have been recognised as a former CAMRA National Pub of the Year, and we continue to serve top-quality ales that draw both locals and ale enthusiasts from across the UK.

Our large beer garden is the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a pint in the sunshine, and we occasionally host lively events like music nights and celebration parties. We welcome everyone—from families to furry friends—to experience our little corner of Welsh hospitality.

Whether you're a local looking for a friendly spot to unwind or a traveller on the hunt for the best real ale, The Bridge End Inn offers a true taste of tradition and community spirit. Come join us and become a part of our story!
